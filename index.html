Data Visualizations

1. Top 15 Cuisine Types in Dublin

Insight: Vegetarian-friendly establishments lead the market (727), followed by European (691) and Irish (658). Cafes rank 8th with 269 establishments, indicating a solid but not oversaturated market segment. The strong showing of vegetarian options suggests health-conscious consumers are a key demographic.

2. Rating Distribution Analysis

Insight: Dublin's restaurant ratings cluster around 4.0 (median), with 36.7% achieving "Excellent" status (4.5+). The distribution shows quality is expected, but there's significant opportunity to stand out by consistently delivering exceptional experiences. Only achieving "Good" (4.0-4.5) places you in a crowded 34.4% segment.

3. Cafe Performance Comparison

Insight: Dublin cafes perform similarly to the overall restaurant market (both averaging ~3.99), suggesting no inherent advantage or disadvantage for cafe concepts. Success depends on execution rather than category. This comparison validates that cafes can compete effectively across all quality tiers.

Methodology

Data Source: TripAdvisor restaurant dataset containing 125,527 restaurants across 31 European cities

Analysis Focus: Dublin market subset (2,082 establishments)

Key Metrics: Cuisine type distribution, rating patterns, price range analysis, cafe-specific performance

Tools: Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn), Google Colab for visualization generation

Limitations: Geographic coordinates not available for spatial analysis; review sentiment not analyzed in detail; seasonal patterns not captured in dataset
